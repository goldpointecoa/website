About Gold Pointe COA

The Gold Pointe Condo Owner's Association is dedicated to maintaining the value, appearance, and quality of our community. Our board works diligently to ensure that Gold Pointe remains a premier residential destination.

Our association handles:

  • Community maintenance and improvements
  • Financial management and budgeting
  • Rules enforcement and community standards
  • Community events and communications
